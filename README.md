## 🎯 Featured Project

### [🩺 Clinical Evidence Copilot](https://github.com/carpasgis-dev/clinical-ai-copilot)

Copiloto experimental de **Epistemic RAG** que integra cohortes estructuradas (SQLite / Synthea) y literatura biomédica real (PubMed / Europe PMC). 
Se diferencia tecnológicamente modelando de forma matemática la jerarquía de la evidencia médica (RCTs > estudios preclínicos).

```text
Patient / cohort context
        ↓
Clinical intent extraction (PICO)
        ↓
Adaptive PubMed retrieval (declarative policy engine)
        ↓
Semantic cross-encoder + Epistemic ranking
        ↓
Applicability scoring & demographic control
        ↓
Hallucination-Constrained Synthesis
```
